## Team Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Research Lead | Defines research directions, validates scientific contributions, supervises manuscripts |
| Project Lead | Manages a specific research direction and coordinates weekly progress |
| Code Lead | Maintains code quality, repository structure, and reproducibility |
| Experiment Lead | Designs and runs experiments, manages logs and result tables |
| Dataset Lead | Manages datasets, annotations, preprocessing, and data documentation |
| Paper Lead | Coordinates paper survey, manuscript writing, figures, and references |
| Presentation Lead | Prepares weekly slides, diagrams, demos, and visual summaries |
